About Smart Fashion Custom Tailor

Smart Fashion Custom Tailor is a premium tailoring service based in Chiang Mai, specialising in bespoke suits, shirts, and custom garments.

Our experienced tailors combine traditional craftsmanship with modern style to deliver perfectly fitted clothing using high-quality fabrics.

Whether you need a business suit, wedding outfit, or casual wear, we provide fast turnaround and exceptional attention to detail for every client.
